# Inquiry for Comprehensive Billing Procedure Details

**Queue:** Billing and Payments
**Priority:** low
**Type:** Request
**Language:** en
**Tags:** Billing, Payment

---

We appreciate your inquiry about the billing process. Invoices are issued at the start of each billing cycle and are sent via email, with payment due within 30 days. Accepted payment methods include credit card, bank transfer, and PayPal. Late payments may attract a fee of 2% per month. For billing errors or disputes, please contact us with supporting information so we can investigate and process any necessary credits or refunds. Feel free to reach out if you need further assistance.
